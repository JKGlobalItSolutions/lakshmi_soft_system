Garden cleaning

  1. Home
  2. Garden cleaning

Service Includes:

  1. Inspection and Assessment:
    • Initial Inspection:
      • Conducting a thorough inspection of your garden to assess its condition.
      • Identifying areas that need cleaning, trimming, or removal of debris.
    • Garden Health Check:
      • Evaluating the health of plants, shrubs, and trees.
      • Providing recommendations for improving garden health and appearance.
  2. Cleaning and Maintenance:
    • Weeding:
      • Removing weeds and unwanted plants from garden beds and pathways.
      • Applying mulch to suppress future weed growth and improve soil health.
    • Debris Removal:
      • Clearing away fallen leaves, branches, and other garden debris.
      • Disposing of waste responsibly to keep your garden tidy.
  3. Trimming and Pruning:
    • Pruning Shrubs and Trees:
      • Trimming overgrown branches to promote healthy growth and improve appearance.
      • Removing dead or diseased branches to prevent spread and maintain plant health.
    • Hedge Trimming:
      • Shaping and maintaining hedges to enhance garden aesthetics.
      • Ensuring even growth and preventing overgrowth.
  4. Lawn Care:
    • Mowing and Edging:
      • Mowing the lawn to the appropriate height for healthy growth.
      • Edging along pathways, flower beds, and driveways for a neat appearance.
    • Aeration and Fertilization:
      • Aerating the lawn to improve soil compaction and root growth.
      • Applying fertilizers to promote lush, green grass.
  5. Plant Care:
    • Planting and Transplanting:
      • Planting new flowers, shrubs, and trees to enhance your garden.
      • Transplanting existing plants to better locations for optimal growth.
    • Watering and Mulching:
      • Ensuring proper watering schedules for all plants.
      • Applying mulch to retain moisture and improve soil quality.
  6. Specialized Services:
    • Pest and Disease Control:
      • Identifying and treating pest infestations and plant diseases.
      • Using eco-friendly methods to protect plants and the environment.
    • Seasonal Cleanup:
      • Providing spring and fall cleanup services to prepare your garden for the changing seasons.
      • Removing seasonal debris and preparing plants for dormancy or growth.
  7. Inspection and Satisfaction Guarantee:
    • Final Inspection:
      • Conducting a thorough inspection of the completed work to ensure quality.
      • Addressing any concerns or additional needs to meet your satisfaction.
    • Customer Satisfaction:
      • Ensuring you are completely satisfied with the finished product.
      • Providing a satisfaction guarantee for peace of mind.
