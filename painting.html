Service Includes:

  1. Surface Preparation:
    • Cleaning and Sanding:
      • Removing dirt, dust, and debris from surfaces to ensure smooth paint application.
      • Sanding surfaces to remove old paint and create a smooth base.
    • Filling Cracks and Holes:
      • Using fillers and putty to repair cracks and holes in walls and ceilings.
      • Smoothing out the repaired areas to blend seamlessly with the rest of the surface.
  2. Priming:
    • Application of Primer:
      • Applying a coat of primer to prepare surfaces for painting.
      • Ensuring better paint adhesion and longevity of the paint job.
  3. Painting:
    • Selection of Paint:
      • Helping choose the right type and color of paint based on your preferences and room usage.
      • Providing options for eco-friendly and low-VOC paints.
    • Application of Paint:
      • Applying paint evenly using brushes, rollers, or sprayers as appropriate.
      • Ensuring even coverage and smooth finish on all surfaces.
  4. Finishing Touches:
    • Touch-Up and Detailing:
      • Touching up any missed spots or imperfections.
      • Detailing edges and corners for a polished look.
    • Cleanup:
      • Removing all painting equipment and materials from the site.
      • Ensuring the area is left clean and tidy.
  5. Additional Services:
    • Wallpaper Removal and Installation:
      • Removing old wallpaper and preparing the surface for painting or new wallpaper.
      • Installing new wallpaper with precision and care.
    • Decorative and Faux Finishes:
      • Applying decorative finishes such as sponging, rag rolling, and stenciling.
      • Creating faux finishes to mimic the appearance of materials like marble or wood.
    • Exterior Painting:
      • Painting the exterior surfaces of homes, including walls, trim, and doors.
      • Using weather-resistant paints to ensure durability and longevity.
  6. Inspection and Satisfaction Guarantee:
    • Final Inspection:
      • Conducting a thorough inspection of the completed work to ensure quality.
      • Addressing any concerns or touch-ups needed to meet your satisfaction.
    • Customer Satisfaction:
      • Ensuring you are completely satisfied with the finished product.
      • Providing a satisfaction guarantee for peace of mind.
