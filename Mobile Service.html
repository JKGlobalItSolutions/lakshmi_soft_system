1. Mobile Installation

Description: This service includes the setup and installation of mobile devices to ensure they are ready for use. Inclusions:

  • Consultation on optimal mobile device selection and configuration
  • Installation of necessary apps and software
  • Configuration of network and internet connections
  • Basic training on using the mobile device

2. Mobile Maintenance

Description: Regular maintenance service to keep your mobile device running smoothly. Inclusions:

  • Inspection of mobile hardware and software functionality
  • Cleaning and optimization of device performance
  • Software updates and system diagnostics
  • Security and firmware updates

3. Mobile Upgrade

Description: Upgrading existing mobile devices to enhance performance and capabilities. Inclusions:

  • Assessment of current mobile device
  • Recommendation of upgrades and new components
  • Installation and configuration of upgraded hardware and software

4. Mobile Repair

Description: This service involves a visit from a technician to inspect and diagnose issues with your mobile device. Inclusions:

  • Thorough inspection of the mobile device
  • Diagnostic of any hardware or software faults
  • Estimate of repair costs if required

5. Mobile Motherboard Chip Level Service

Description: Expert repair service for mobile device motherboards at the chip level to ensure your devices run smoothly. Inclusions:

  • Detailed inspection and diagnostics of motherboard issues
  • Chip-level repairs and replacements
  • Testing and validation of repairs
